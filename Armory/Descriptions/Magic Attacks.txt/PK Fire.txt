PK Fire, PSI Fire in EarthBound, is an offensive PSI ability that appears in every game in the Mother series. PK Fire is realized in EarthBound Beginnings by Ana, in EarthBound by Paula, and in Mother 3 by Kumatora. PK Fire is usually effective on plant-like and ice enemies.
