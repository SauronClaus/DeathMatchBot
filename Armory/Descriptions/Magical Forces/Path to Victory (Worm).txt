The user is capable of choosing from a certain pathway of choices, a number which will ensure the highest likelihood of making them victorious in any kind of outcome. The user's power supplies them with whatever steps they need to take at the task they visualize; whether leading, governing, planning, combat, communicating, psychological manipulation, inventing, etc, or even flat out environment control through use of super-powered pawns. The user merely has to pick their conceptual desire, and will find the series of steps required to attaining their path to the victory.
