The Satsui no Hado ("Surge of Murderous Intent" or "Surge of Killing Intent"), sometimes translated as the Dark Hado, is a dark energy-based force that is known for its use by Akuma. It is the antithesis of the Power of Nothingness, which is mainly used by Gouken and Ryu.
