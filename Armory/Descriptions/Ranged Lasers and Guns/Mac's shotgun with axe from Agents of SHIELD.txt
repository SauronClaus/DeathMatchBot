The Shotgun-Axe is a weapon crafted by Alphonso Mackenzie to be used as both a firearm and a melee weapon. Mackenzie completed it in time to fight Hive's Primitives on the Zephyr One, which is capable of firing normal and I.C.E.R. shells.
