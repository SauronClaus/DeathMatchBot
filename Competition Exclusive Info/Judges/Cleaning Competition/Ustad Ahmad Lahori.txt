Ustad Ahmad Lahori was an architect in present day India during Mughal Empire. It is said that he was the chief architect of the Taj Mahal in Agra, India, built between 1632 and 1648 during the ruling period of Mughal emperor Shah Jahan. Its architecture, being a combination of Persian and Mughal architectural styles, is widely praised around the world and Taj Mahal is considered to be one of the 'wonders of the world'.
