Alexandra Maria Guarnaschelli is an executive chef at New York City's Butter restaurant and was executive chef at The Darby restaurant before its closing. She appears as a television personality on the Food Network shows The Kitchen, Chopped, Iron Chef America, All Star Family Cook-off, Guy's Grocery Games, and The Best Thing I Ever Ate. She hosts Alex's Day Off, The Cooking Loft and Supermarket Stakeout on Food Network and Cooking Channel. In 2012, she won that season of The Next Iron Chef: Redemption.
