Players have to catch as many fruits as possible. They have to avoid getting touched by Urchins. They are on a waterfall. The more fruits they collect, the more points they earn. However, if they touch an Urchin, they drop fruits and lose 3 points. If the player falls off the waterfall, they lose time and 3 points. The player gets the most points wins the minigame.
