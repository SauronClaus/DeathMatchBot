No interference with other competitiors is allowed.
Each competitor must follow all the rules of Losing chess.
Capturing is compulsory.
When more than one capture is available, the player may choose.
The king has no royal power and so, may be captured as any other piece (without check or checkmate), can expose itself, there is no castling, and a pawn can promote to king.
Stalemate is a win for the stalemated player (the player with no legal moves).
