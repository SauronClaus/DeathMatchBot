The winner is whoever wins in the Electoral College.
No interfering directly with the competitor's election; indirect interference, such as mud slinging, is allowed.
No rigging of the results, through means such as bribery, is allowed. Claims of bribery or other illegal activities is allowed.
Both candidates are magically allowed to run for the presidency; however, if they are later disqualified during the race, they automatically lose.
The election takes place during the most recent presidential election cycle.
