Toshinori Yagi (八木俊典 Yagi Toshinori?), also known as All Might (オールマイト Ōru Maito?), is the former No. 1 Pro Hero who bore the title of the world's Symbol of Peace (平和の象徴 Heiwa no Shōchō?). He teaches Foundational Hero Studies at U.A. High School.
