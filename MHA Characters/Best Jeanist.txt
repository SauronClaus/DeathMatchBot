Tsunagu Hakamada (袴田維 Hakamada Tsunagu?),[1] also known as Fiber Hero: Best Jeanist (ファイバーヒーロー ベストジー二スト Faibā Hīrō Besuto Jīnisuto?), is the former No. 4, and the current No. 3 Pro Hero, as well as the recipient of the "Best Jeanist" award for eight consecutive years.
