Kaoruko Awata (泡田薫子 Awata Kaoruko?),[2] also known as Bubble Girl (バブルガール Baburu Gāru?),[3] is a Pro Hero and sidekick. She worked at Sir Nighteye's hero office as a sidekick along with Centipeder, until Sir Nighteye passed away. She still works at the same office, just as one of Centipeder's (the new boss) sidekicks.
