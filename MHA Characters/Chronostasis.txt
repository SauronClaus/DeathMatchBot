Hari Kurono (玄野針 Kurono Hari?),[1] also known as Chronostasis (クロノスタシス Kuronosutashisu?), is a member of the Yakuza and the assistant of Overhaul in the Shie Hassaikai. He is the secondary antagonist of the Shie Hassaikai Arc.
