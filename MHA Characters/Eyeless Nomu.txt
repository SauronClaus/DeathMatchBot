Hosu Nomu (脳無(保須市襲撃) Hosu-shi Shūgeki Nōmu?) are a trio of Nomu summoned by Tomura Shigaraki to wreak havoc on Hosu City. They are some of the main antagonists of the Vs. Hero Killer Arc.
