Flect Turn (フレクト・ターン Furekuto Tān?) is a villain and the main antagonist for My Hero Academia: World Heroes' Mission. He is the leader of the global cult Humarise, which subscribes to the Quirk Singularity Doomsday Theory and aims to eliminate Quirks from the world.
