Fuyumi Todoroki (轟冬美 Todoroki Fuyumi?) is the older sister of Shoto and Natsuo, the younger sister of Toya, and the only daughter of Enji and Rei Todoroki.[3] She is also a teacher at an unnamed elementary school.
