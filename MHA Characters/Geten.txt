Geten (外典 Geten?), surname Himura (氷叢 Himura?),[2] also known as Iceman (氷男 Kōriotoko?), is a villain who was a member of the Meta Liberation Army, and one of the main antagonists of the Meta Liberation Army Arc. Later, when the Liberation Army and League of Villains came together to form the Paranormal Liberation Front, he becomes one of its nine lieutenants.
