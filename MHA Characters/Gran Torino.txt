Sorahiko Torino (酉野空彦 Torino Sorahiko?),[1] also known by his hero name Gran Torino (グラントリノ Guran Torino?), is a retired Pro Hero, formerly a homeroom teacher at U.A. High School,[2] and a mentor to Toshinori Yagi and Izuku Midoriya.
