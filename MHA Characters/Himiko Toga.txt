Himiko Toga (渡我被身子 Toga Himiko?)[2] is a major antagonist of the My Hero Academia manga and anime series. She was a member of the League of Villains, affiliated with the Vanguard Action Squad,[3] and later becoming one of the nine lieutenants of the Paranormal Liberation Front.
