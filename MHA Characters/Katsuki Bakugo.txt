Katsuki Bakugo (爆豪勝己 Bakugō Katsuki?), also known as Kacchan (かっちゃん Katchan?) by his childhood friends,[2] and his hero name Great Explosion Murder God Dynamight (大•爆•殺•神ダイナマイト Daibakusasshin Dainamaito?),[3] is a student in Class 1-A at U.A. High School, training to become a Pro Hero. He is the deuteragonist of the series.
