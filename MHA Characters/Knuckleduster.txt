Iwao Oguro (雄黒巌 Oguro Iwao?)[3], commonly known as Master (師匠 Shishō?), is the vigilante known as Knuckleduster (ナックルダスター Nakkurudasutā?). He was formerly the Pro Hero High-Speed Hero: O'Clock (超速ヒーロー・オクロック Chōsoku Hīrō Okurokku?) in Japan, and Hyper Quadfist (超頻四拳 Chaopin sūchen?) in China.
