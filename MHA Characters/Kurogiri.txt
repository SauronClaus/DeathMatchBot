Kurogiri (黒霧 Kurogiri?, lit. "Black Fog") is a major antagonist in the series. He is a B-rank villain and a High-End Nomu[1] created by Doctor Kyudai Garaki from the corpse of Oboro Shirakumo to serve and protect Tomura Shigaraki under All For One's command.
