Dr. Kyudai Garaki (殻木球大 Garaki Kyūdai?),[2] also known by the pseudonyms Daruma Ujiko (氏子達磨 Ujiko Daruma?)[3][4] and Dr. Tsubasa (翼 Tsubasa?),[5] is a villain affiliated with the League of Villains. He is a faithful servant of All For One and later to Tomura Shigaraki.
