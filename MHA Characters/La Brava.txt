Manami Aiba (相場愛美 Aiba Manami?), also known as La Brava, (ラブラバ Raburaba?), is a former villain associated with Gentle. She is a side antagonist of the U.A. School Festival Arc. During the Final War, Manami sides with the Heroes, aiding Naomasa Tsukauchi and the Counter-Force in fighting the Paranormal Liberation Front.[4]
