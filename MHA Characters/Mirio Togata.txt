Mirio Togata (通形ミリオ Tōgata Mirio?), also known as Lemillion (ルミリオン Rumirion?), is a student in Class 3-B[2] at U.A. High School and is part of the The Big 3. Mirio was originally the top candidate to become All Might's successor and the inheritor of One For All before he met Izuku Midoriya.
