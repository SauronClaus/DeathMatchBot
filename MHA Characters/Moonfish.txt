Moonfish (ムーンフィッシュ Mūnfisshu?) is a villain affiliated with the League of Villains and also part of its Vanguard Action Squad. He and the rest of his team serve as the main antagonists of the Forest Training Camp Arc. After being defeated and arrested, he was sent to Tartarus prison, where he awaited execution,[2] until he was freed by All For One.[3] He was defeated and arrested again during the Final War.
