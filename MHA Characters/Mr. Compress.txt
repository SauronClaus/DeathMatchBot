Atsuhiro Sako (迫圧紘 Sako Atsuhiro?), also known as Mr. Compress (Mr.コンプレス Misutā Konpuresu?), is a recurring villain affiliated with the League of Villains, was a member of the organization's Vanguard Action Squad, and later became one of the nine lieutenants of the Paranormal Liberation Front.
