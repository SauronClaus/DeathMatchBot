Number 6 (No. 6 Nanbā Shikkusu?),[5] also known as Rokuro Nomura (野村六郎 Nomura Rokurō)[6] or the Scarred Man (傷顔の男 Kizugao no Otoko?),[7] was a member of the Villain Factory and the main antagonist of My Hero Academia: Vigilantes. He was also a Pro Hero under the alias Rock (ロック Rokku?)[8] or High-Speed Hero: O'Clock II (超速ヒーロー オクロックII Chōsoku Hīrō Okurokku Tsū?).
