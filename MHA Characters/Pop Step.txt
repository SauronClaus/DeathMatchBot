Kazuho Haneyama (羽根山和歩 Haneyama Kazuho?) is a former Vigilante and freelance idol, known as Pop☆Step (ポップ☆ステップ Poppu☆Suteppu?) or simply Pop (ポップ Poppu?). She is one of the protagonists of My Hero Academia: Vigilantes.
