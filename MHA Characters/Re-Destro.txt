Rikiya Yotsubashi (四ツ橋力也 Yotsubashi Rikiya?),[1] also known by his alias Re-Destro (リ・デストロ Ri-Desutoro?), is the President and CEO of the Detnerat Company, and was also the Grand Commander of the modern Meta Liberation Army, which he inherited as the descendant of the infamous villain Destro.[2] He was the primary antagonist of the Meta Liberation Army Arc.
