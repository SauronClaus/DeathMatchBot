Chiyo Shuzenji (修善寺治与 Shūzenji Chiyo?),[1] also known as Youthful Heroine: Recovery Girl (妙齢ヒロイン リカバリーガール Myōrei Hiroin Rikabarī Gāru?),[2] is a nurse at U.A. High School. She has two offices; one located in U.A. and one in the Sports Festival Stadium.
