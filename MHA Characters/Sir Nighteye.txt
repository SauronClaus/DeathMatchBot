Mirai Sasaki (佐々木未来 Sasaki Mirai?),[1] also known as Sir Nighteye (サー・ナイトアイ Sā Naitoai?) was a Pro Hero and Mirio Togata's mentor. He was also formerly a Sidekick to All Might and considered the brains behind him.
