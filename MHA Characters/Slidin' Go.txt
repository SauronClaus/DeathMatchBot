Tatsuyuki Tokoname (常滑達行 Tokoname Tatsuyuki?), also known as Slidin' Go (スライディン・ゴー Suraidin Gō?), is a former Pro Hero, a member of the Meta Liberation Army, and later the Paranormal Liberation Front as one of the 2nd-ranked advisers in the Vanguard Action Intelligence Regiment: Carmine when the Meta Liberation Army and the League of Villains merged.
