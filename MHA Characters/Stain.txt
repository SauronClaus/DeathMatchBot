Chizome Akaguro (赤黒血染 Akaguro Chizome?), also known as Hero Killer: Stain (ヒーロー殺しステイン Hīrō Goroshi Sutein?), and formerly Stendhal (スタンダール Sutandāru?), was a villain and former vigilante that was notorious for having killed many Pro Heroes. He is the main antagonist in the titular Vs. Hero Killer Arc.
